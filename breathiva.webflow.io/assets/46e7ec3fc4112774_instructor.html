FAQ’s
FAQ’s

Everything you need to know before joining our Yoga studio and starting your practice

What should I bring to a Yoga class?

Bring a Yoga mat, a water bottle, and a small towel. Wear comfortable, stretchable clothing that allows easy movement.

How often should I practice Yoga?

Practicing two to four times per week helps build strength, flexibility, and consistency.

Are your classes suitable for all age groups?

Yes, our classes are designed for all age levels. We offer beginner-friendly sessions as well as advanced practices.

What types of Yoga classes do you offer?

We offer Hatha, Vinyasa, Power Yoga, Restorative Yoga, and Meditation sessions to suit different goals and experience levels.

Do you offer trial classes or drop-in sessions?

Yes, we provide trial classes and flexible drop-in options so you can experience our sessions before committing.

Do you offer private or one-on-one sessions?

Yes, we offer personalized one-on-one sessions tailored to your goals, schedule, and fitness level.
